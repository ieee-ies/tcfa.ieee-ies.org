Books

The members of the TC on Factory Automation have published several books relevant to the areas of the TC.

Mechatronics and Robotics - New Trends and Challenges [2020]
Aim of the book is to offer a wide overview of new research trends and challenges for both mechatronics and robotics, through the contribution of researchers from different institutions, providing their view on specific subjects they consider as “hot topics” in both fields, with attention to new fields of application, new challenges to the research communities and new technologies available.
